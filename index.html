My name is Catherine Sosa Lazaro and I am a first year Theater, Film and Digital Production major at University of California Riverside. My concentration is in film making and I am especially interested in screenwriting specifically. However, my concentration is in film making to be well-rounded in all aspects of the film industry. Many in this department are encouraged to be well rounded in different aspects of theater and film in which one acquires skill sets in writing, filming and acting.
